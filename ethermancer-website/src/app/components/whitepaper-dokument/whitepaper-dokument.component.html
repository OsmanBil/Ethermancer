Ethermancer Whitepaper

Executive Summary

Ethermancer is an Ethereum trading bot leveraging multiple analysis strategies, such as moving averages, Bollinger Bands, and RSI, along with artificial intelligence. It automatically analyzes, buys, and sells to optimize your trading experience.

1. Introduction

Ethermancer is designed to bridge the gap between advanced and novice traders in the Ethereum market. With artificial intelligence and advanced algorithms, Ethermancer allows users to make smart trading decisions with minimal effort.
