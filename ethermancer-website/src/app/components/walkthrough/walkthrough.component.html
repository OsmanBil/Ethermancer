How to Get Started with Ethermancer

Step 1: Sign up for an Ethermancer account. Visit our website and click the 'Sign Up' button. Complete the registration form and verify your email.

Step 2: Connect Your Wallet. Go to 'Settings' and connect your Ethereum wallet to Ethermancer.

Step 3: Configure Trading Preferences. Navigate to the 'Dashboard' and set your trading preferences like stop-loss, take-profit, and risk level.

Step 4: Test the Bot. Use our simulator to test the bot's performance based on historical data. Adjust your settings accordingly.

Step 5: Start Trading. Once you're satisfied with the bot's performance in the simulator, you can start live trading. Activate the bot, and Ethermancer will take care of the rest.
